## ★★★ PRIMARY SOURCES (already referenced in book plan)

### Spectrum Expert #01 (X-Trade, 1997)
- **Platform:** Pentagon 128
- **Key articles:** Programming (fast 3D graphics), Programming Algorithms, STORM assembler guide, PRO-TRACKER 3.01, GS sound system, GMX hardware
- **Authors:** Dark (code + articles), Dangerous (cover)
- **Music:** 9 compositions by Frunze (PT2)
- **Download:** SP_EXP1.ZIP (TRD) or SP_EXP1O.zip (UDI)
- **Rating:** 4.08/5

### Spectrum Expert #02 (X-Trade, 1998)
- **Platform:** Pentagon 128
- **Key articles:** Hardware, Programming (Pascal/C compilers), ZX Spectrum hardware mods, Sprinter opinion, Music
- **Authors:** Dark & Dangerous
- **Download:** SP_EXP2.ZIP (TRD) or SP_EXP2O.zip (UDI)
- **Rating:** 4.14/5

## ★★☆ HIGH VALUE — Check for technical content

### ZX Format (XL-Design / Logros, 1995-1997, 8+ issues)
- Issues #01-#08 all on ZXArt
- **#02:** Assembler intro (flags, arithmetic, registers), turbo modes, ZX General Sound by X-Trade
- **#04-#07:** "Golden period" — likely most technical content
- **#4.5:** ENLiGHT'96 special (the party where Illusion won 1st place)

### Born Dead (newspaper, ~2000-2001)
- **#0F:** CC000 compo rules, CC.999.999 results, tracker editor overview, scene critique
- **#10:** Forever 2e3, Paradox Party, Mekka Symposium 2000 reports
- Atmospheric scene reporting, good for Ch.15 (Living Demoscene)

### Black Crow #05 (2001)
- **★ Contains multicolor article with algorithm and example code**
- Also: TR-DOS variables, hardware projects (cache, disk controllers)
- Platform: ZX Spectrum 48K

### Subliminal Extacy (Raww Arse → Papaya Dezign, 1995-2024)
- **Rare ENGLISH-LANGUAGE ZX zine!**
- **#06 (2024):** Hardware maintenance, ASCII art, community interviews, game "Blumps"
- **#04 (2021), #03 (2001):** Check for coding content
- Multi-format releases (TAP, esxDOS, divMMC)

### Deja Vu #0A (Playgear Company, 2000)
- Praised as one of the best journals. Check for technical articles.

## Access Notes

- Most magazines available as TRD disk images, viewable in:
  - Unreal Speccy (best for Soviet formats)
  - ZEsarUX
  - ZXMAK2
- Some also available as TAP or SCL
- ZXArt sometimes has article text extracted and indexed (searchable)
- vtrdos.ru is another source for TRD images

## Search Strategy on ZXArt

Search keywords to find coding articles across all journals:
- `мультиколор` / `multicolor`
- `ротозум` / `rotozoomer`
- `3D` / `вращение` / `rotation`
- `таблица синусов` / `sine table`
- `256 байт` / `4K` / `size coding`
- `AY` / `digital drums` / `beeper`
- `making of` / `как делалось`
- `ассемблер` / `assembler` / `кодинг`
